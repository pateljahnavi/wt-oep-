KESARI(HINDI)

Summary

Time: 2 hr 30 min

        Set in the 19th century, Kesari recounts the story of Havildar Ishar Singh who played a key role in the 1897 Battle of 
        Saragarhi,in which an army of 21 Sikhs fought against a legion of Afghans in what is considered as one of the greatest 
        last-stands in military history.
