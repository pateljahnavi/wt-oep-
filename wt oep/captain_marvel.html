CAPTAIN MARVEL(ENGLISH)

Summary


        Time: 2 hr 04 min

    Set in the 1990s, Marvel Studios` "Captain Marvel" is an all-new adventure from a previously unseen
    period in the history of the Marvel Cinematic Universe that follows the journey of Carol Danvers as
    she becomes one of the universe`s most powerful heroes. While a galactic war between two alien races 
    reaches Earth, Danvers finds herself and a small cadre of allies at the center of the maelstrom.
